# Job Application System — Step by Step

> 340+ random applications = 0 calls. That approach is dead. This is the new system.

---

## Why Zero Calls? (Brutal Truth)

**LinkedIn Easy Apply:** 500-2000 people apply per role. Recruiter sees top 20 (sorted by LinkedIn algorithm). You're invisible.

**Naukri Mass Apply:** Same problem. Plus Naukri is flooded with non-relevant applicants. Recruiters have stopped checking most roles there.

**What actually works in 2026:** Direct outreach to humans. Recruiter messages. Referrals. Company career pages. Platforms where recruiters come to YOU.

---

## The New System

### Step 1: Set Up Profiles (Do This ONCE, Today)

**Instahyre** (instahyre.com)
- Create profile. Add ALL these keywords: GenAI, LLM, LangChain, LangGraph, AWS Bedrock, RAG, Multi-Agent Systems, Python, Terraform, Docker, FastAPI
- Upload resume PDF
- Recruiters search by keywords and reach out to YOU
- Less competition than LinkedIn/Naukri — recruiters are more active here
- 560+ GenAI jobs in Bangalore on Instahyre right now (April 2026)

**Cutshort** (cutshort.io)
- Startup-focused. Many AI startups in Bangalore hire through Cutshort.
- Same keywords as above
- Set salary expectation: ₹12-18 LPA

**Wellfound** (wellfound.com, formerly AngelList)
- Best for AI/ML startup roles
- Salary ranges visible upfront — filter for ₹10-20 LPA
- Filter: Bangalore, AI/ML/GenAI

**LinkedIn Profile (Fix It)**
- Headline: "GenAI Developer | LangGraph | AWS Bedrock | RAG | Multi-Agent Systems"
- NOT: "Looking for opportunities" or "Open to work" (desperate signal)
- About section: 3-4 lines about what you BUILD, not what you want
- Turn ON "Open to recruiters" in settings (different from "Open to work" badge)

### Step 2: Daily Application Process (30 min/day)

**Find 2-3 jobs to apply to. Sources:**
1. Instahyre/Cutshort/Wellfound notifications
2. Company career pages directly (see target list below)
3. LinkedIn job search (but DON'T use Easy Apply)

**For EACH job, follow this exact process:**

```
1. Find the job posting
2. Go to the COMPANY'S OWN career page → Apply there directly
3. Find the recruiter or hiring manager on LinkedIn
   - Search: "[Company] recruiter AI" or "[Company] talent acquisition"
   - Or: "[Company] engineering manager"
4. Send them a message (template below)
5. Log it in your tracker
6. Move to next job
```

**To Employee (for referral):**
```
Hi [Name],

I noticed you work at [Company]. I'm a GenAI developer

workflows (LangGraph), and a real-time voice bot on AWS.

I'm interested in the [Role Title] position. Would you

Amit
```

**Follow-up (after 7 days, ONE time only):**
```
Hi [Name], following up on my message about the [Role] 
position. Still very interested. Let me know if you need 
any additional info. Thanks!
```

**Minimum weekly targets:**

## Salary Negotiation (When You Get an Offer)

**When asked "What's your expected CTC?"**
"Based on my production GenAI experience and the market rate for GenAI engineers in Bangalore, I'm targeting 14-16 LPA. I'm flexible based on the overall package and growth opportunities."

Why 14-16 when you want 12? They'll counter at 11-12, you negotiate to 12-13.

**When you get an offer:**
- NEVER accept immediately. "Can I have 2-3 days to review?"
- If below target: "I was expecting closer to [X]. Is there flexibility?"
- Mention other discussions: "I'm in conversations with a couple of other companies."

**Bond discussion (only when offer is in hand):**
"I have a transition obligation. Would the company be able to support with a signing bonus?"
Many companies have signing bonus budgets. ₹2L is small for them.

**NEVER mention:**
- Current CTC (say "I'd prefer to discuss based on market rate")
- Bond amount upfront
- Anything negative about Rapyder

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Applying without messaging anyone** — Your resume goes into a black hole
2. **Sending generic messages** — Customize with role title and company name
3. **Applying to 50 jobs in one sitting** — Quality over quantity. 3 targeted > 50 random
4. **Waiting to be "ready"** — You're ready NOW for ₹12-15L GenAI roles
5. **Only applying to dream companies** — Apply to Tier 3 too. Interview practice is valuable.
6. **Not following up** — One follow-up after 7 days. Many recruiters respond to follow-ups.
7. **Mentioning current salary** — Anchors you low. Always talk market rate.
